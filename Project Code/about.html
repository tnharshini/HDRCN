About OptiText

OptiText is a cutting-edge handwritten text recognition platform. Powered by advanced AI, we transform messy or complex handwritten input into clean, editable digital text.

Our Mission

We aim to make handwritten content as usable and accessible as printed text. With OptiText, you can scan, interpret, and convert notes, forms, and documents in seconds.

Key Features

• Supports a wide range of handwriting styles and languages
• Converts text into audio for better accessibility
• Simple upload-and-process workflow
• Optimized for both desktop and mobile use

Why Choose OptiText?

Unlike traditional OCR tools, OptiText focuses on handwritten input, trained with modern machine learning models. Whether you’re a student, teacher, archivist, or researcher, we’re here to bridge the gap between analog and digital.

Built with Purpose

OptiText combines cutting-edge AI with elegant interface design. Our goal is to provide a fast, user-friendly experience while maintaining high recognition accuracy. From casual use to large-scale digitization, we're ready for it all.
